# How to Play SketchIt

## Game Overview

SketchIt is a fun multiplayer drawing and guessing game where players take turns drawing and guessing words.

## How to Play

1. **Join a match** and wait for another player to join.
2. **One player receives a secret word** to draw on the canvas.
3. **The other player guesses** the word in real time using the chat box.
4. **Earn points** for correct guesses and fast drawings.
5. **The game alternates roles** each round, so everyone gets to draw and guess.

## Tips for Success

- Use **smooth strokes** and keep your drawings simple
- **Communicate clearly** in the chat when guessing
- **Practice makes perfect** - the more you play, the better you'll get!

## Scoring

- Points are awarded for correct guesses
- Faster correct guesses earn more points
- Drawing quickly and accurately also contributes to your score
